Our Innermost for His Utmost

This collective is more than clothing.

Guts and Glory is a call.

We are taking our innermost and using it for His utmost.

We weren't called to a life of comfort, we were created to challenge contentment, convenience, and complacency.

Life's a party when you know Jesus - he's the one who turned water into wine.

Walking alongside him means we already have the victory.

What would you do if you had no fear?

What would you do if you knew you wouldn't fail?

The victory is yours - what are you going to do with it?

Backstory

Imagine how different life would be if you had nothing to fear.

We fear what others think of us. We fear the unknowns that come with new seasons, we fear trying new things with the chance we might fail.

Who would you be if you weren't afraid of anything?

There is this dude that enjoys breaking the rules and walks everywhere barefoot. He sits with strangers and goes against society. This guy tells people what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. He decided not to play it safe - he risked his entire reputation.

Who would you be if you weren't afraid of anything?

He is radical.

He is a rebel.

He is Jesus.
